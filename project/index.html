A project about all energy usage and production of European countries using data visualizations in D3.

Introduction

We are nearing the time where coal-fired power plants are closing en masse in the Netherlands. Large amounts of wind farms are manifesting in the North Sea, with many on the planning. What's even better: starting from 2022, subsidy is not needed anymore for energy companies to build financially advantageous wind farms.

But, what about these statistics at European scale when we scrutinize them? What is the (renewable) energy consumption and production? And over the years? Is our little "kikkerlandje" doing well?
